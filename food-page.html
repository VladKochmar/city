About Moby Dick Restaurant

Fish restaurant "El Halaka" is located in Hurghada, not far from the port mosque of El Mina and opposite the fish market. The interior of the two-story restaurant is made in simple forms. On the first floor, the attention of visitors is attracted by two aquariums. It offers traditional Egyptian fresh seafood dishes: fish soups, fillets, mussels, grilled crabs and lobsters, several types of shrimp, fish salads with vegetables and spices, shrimp cocktails and many other delicious dishes. On the ground floor there is a display case with fresh fish on ice, which will be prepared for you on the spot for a meal in one of the halls, or to enjoy a meal at home.

Highlights
  • Choose from a variety of menu options
  • Get access to a lively party with the finest local DJs
  • Enjoy a delicious dinner
